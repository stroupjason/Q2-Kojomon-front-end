How to Play

Kojomon is your virtual plant pet. To play, just create an account!

Kojomon need love and care, in order to grow.

Your Kojomon will need food, attention, and to be kept clean of pests.

To feed your Kojomon, select 'Feed Your Kojo,' then click on the plant! Do the same for attention and cleaning.

Keep track of your Kojomon's health by looking at the progress bars in the upper right corner.

How you care for your Kojomon determines what form your plant will evovle into. If you neglect your Kojomon too much, it will wither away!

Collect badges as you play!
